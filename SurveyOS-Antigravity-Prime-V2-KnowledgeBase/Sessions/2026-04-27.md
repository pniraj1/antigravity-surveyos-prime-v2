## Key Decisions

> [!tip] Key Takeaways
> - Slice typing uses `StateCreator<any, any, any, SliceType>` to avoid circular imports; full type safety enforced at `create<ClaimState>()` composition site.
> - `BillCheckGrid` owns its own column-visibility and row-selection state (not lifted to parent) to avoid prop drilling.
> - `aiDataSlice` contains all 10 document-type mapping functions (RC, policy, DL, claim, permit, auth, fitness, loka challan, final bill, estimate).
> - Windows Python: `py` launcher is the working command — `python` and `python3` both fail on this machine.
